Key Lime Cake

Total Time: 1 hr 10 min Makes: 1 3-layer (8-inch) cake Author: Judy ???

Cake:

  • 1 (15.25 ounce) package lemon cake mix
  • 1 ⅓ cups vegetable oil
  • ¾ cup orange juice
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 (3 ounce) package lime flavored Jell-O mix

Frosting:

  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • ½ cup butter
  • 4 cups confectioners' sugar
  • 3 tablespoons fresh lime juice

Directions

Step 1

Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease three 8-inch round cake pans.

Step 2

To make the cake: Beat cake mix, oil, orange juice, eggs, and gelatin mix in a large bowl until well combined.

Step 3

Pour into the prepared cake pans.

Step 4

Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cake layer comes out clean, 16 to 18 minutes. Allow cake layers to cool completely.

Step 5

Meanwhile, make frosting: Beat cream cheese and butter in a large bowl until light and fluffy.

Step 6

Add confectioners' sugar and lime juice; mix until well combined.

Step 7

Fill and frost cooled cake layers.


Cheddar Bay Biscuits

Total Time: 20 min Makes: 1 dozen Author: Lisa Longley

Biscuits:

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon table salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup mild or medium cheddar
  • 1 cup buttermilk

Butter Topping:

  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

Directions

Step 1

Preheat your oven to 450 degrees Fahrenheit. Line a rimmed baking sheet with parchment paper.

Step 2

In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, sugar, garlic powder and salt.

Step 3

Using a fork or pastry cutter, cut the butter into the flour mixture. It will be crumbly.

Step 4

Add in the cheese and buttermilk. Stir together until just combined. Using a 3 tablespoon cookie scoop or a large spoon, scoop out the biscuits and place them approximately 2 inches apart on the prepared baking sheet. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes or until golden brown.

Step 5

While the biscuits are baking, whisk together the ingredients for the butter topping. As soon as the biscuits come out of the oven, brush them with the butter topping. Serve warm.


Easy Broccoli Cheddar Soip

Total Time: 30 min Makes: 6 servings Author: Lisa Longley

Cake:

  • 3 tablespoons unsalted buter
  • 2 cups shredded carrot
  • 1 small yellow onion
  • 1 teasponn kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/8 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/3 cup all purpose flour
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 4 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 1/2 cups half and half
  • 2 cups mild cheddar cheese

Directions

Step 1

Melt the butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onions and carrots. Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg. Sautee for three to four minutes until the onions are tender.

Step 2

Whisk in the flour, and cook for about 30 seconds.

Step 3

Very slowly pour in the chicken stock. Add about two tablespoons at a time, fully whisking it in and combining it before adding more. As you add more, you will be able to add more quickly.

Step 4

Stir in the broccoli, bring to a simmer, and cook for 15 minutes or until the broccoli is soft and tender.

Step 5

Optional: Remove some of the soup and blend it for a less chunky soup.

Step 6

Stir in the half and half and allow the soup to warm through.

Step 7

Stir in the chedder cheese until it is fully melted and combined into the soup.
