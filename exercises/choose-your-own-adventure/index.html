The Adventure to the Rare Emerald

In the evening, you and your team of five other friends get together for what seems like a regular hangout, but little do any of you know, you're all in for a wild adventure. There's info of a rare gem that occupies a mysterious base downtown, and a set of antagonists are also after it! If it gets into their hands, this could spell trouble for the entire city! After learning about this, you all head out into the sunset to meet with someone who's very skilled in these matters to help lead your team to the emerald!

Let's go!
